Here's a timeline of Kobe's NBA career:

  • (1996) Draft Day-

    Kobe Bryant is drafted to the Charlotte Hornets at the age of 17 and then traded to the Los Angeles Lakers.

  • (1997) Slam Dunk Champion-

    Up against, Ray Allen, Chris Carr, Michael Finley, Darvin Ham, and Bob Sura, Kobe Bryant wins the Slam Dunk Championship at the age of 18.

  • (2000)Kobe wins his first NBA Championship-

    Kobe wins his first NBA Championship with the Lakers alongside Shaquille O'neal.

  • (2001)Second NBA Championship-

    Kobe wins his second NBA Championship with the Lakers against Allen Iverson and the Sixers.

  • (2002)3-Peat Champions

    The Lakers continue on to win their 14th overall chamoionship and Kobe's 3rd against the New Jersey Nets.

  • (2006) Career-high 81 points-

    Kobe Bryant demonstrates how well of a scorer he is making 81 points in a single game.

  • (2008) Wins Gold with Team USA

    Team USA becomes World Champions along side fellow NBA Champions: LeBron James, Dwyane Wade, Jason Kidd, Carlos Boozer...

  • (2009) Forth Championship

    The Lakers triumph over Orlando with the help of the newly added Pau Gasol.

  • (2010) Fifth Championship-

    The Lakers beat Celtics in game 7 earning Kobe's fifth and final ring.

  • (2012) Second Olympic Medal-

    Team USA becomes World Champions with new stars: Kevin Durant, Russel Westbrook, James Harden, Anthony Davis...

  • (2013) Kobe suffers torn Achilles Injury

    Kobe gets hurt on his 17th season in the league which then continues on with a knee injury the following season as Kobe begins to feel the wear and tear on his body.

  • (2016) Kobe Retires from Basketball-

    On November 29, 2015 Kobe publishes a poem "Dear Basketball" to annouce his retirement at the end of the season. April 13, 2016, he plays his final game of the season and scores season-high 60 points to end his career.
